About Search for the Rift Walker

Search for the Rift Walker is a LitRPG web serial that asks a simple question: What would you do to find your son?

Ray is a father torn from his son Riker by a dimensional rift. Armed with nothing but determination and a d20, he traverses impossible worlds, facing dangers both mundane and cosmic. But here's the twist: he doesn't control the outcome. A real d20 roll determines his fate at every turn.

This isn't a story about a hero. It's a story about love, persistence, and the willingness to accept uncertainty in pursuit of something that matters.
