# NWA Red Iron Forge - Mobile Personal Training

Professional landing page for Cody's mobile personal training business in Northwest Arkansas.

## Features

- **Professional Design** - Strong, credible branding
- **Mobile Service Focus** - Emphasizes unique value prop
- **Client Testimonials** - Real 5-star reviews
- **Clear Pricing** - $75/hour with full details
- **Services Overview** - All specialties listed
- **Easy Contact** - Simple booking process
- **Mobile Responsive** - Perfect on all devices

## Deployment to Netlify

1. **Go to netlify.com**
2. **Drag this folder** (`nwaredironforge/`) into the Netlify dashboard
3. **Done** - You get a live URL instantly

## Customization

### Update Contact Information
Edit these in `index.html`:
- Phone number (search for "tel:")
- Contact method details
- Any service descriptions

### Colors
To adjust the professional palette:
- Edit CSS variables at top of `css/style.css`
- Primary navy: #1B3A66
- Accent red: #C41E3A

### Services & Testimonials
All text is easily editable directly in the HTML. Update to match your current offerings or client feedback.

## About This Design

**Why these choices:**
- Navy/red professional palette = strength, credibility, trust
- "We bring the workout to you" = immediate value prop
- 10 years + 8 years business = establishes expertise
- Testimonials = social proof from real clients
- Service cards = shows versatility
- Clear pricing = transparency builds trust

**Target conversion:** "This trainer has credibility, is experienced, and brings training to my home"

## Support

Need changes? Edit the HTML/CSS directly, test locally, redeploy to Netlify.
