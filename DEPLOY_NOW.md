# Deploy to Netlify in 2 Minutes

## Step 1: Go to Netlify
Visit https://netlify.com

## Step 2: Sign In
- Click "Log in" (use your existing account if you have one)
- Or "Sign up" free with GitHub/email

## Step 3: Deploy
- Find the "Drag and drop" area on the dashboard
- Drag the entire `quickaffordablesites` folder into it
- Wait 30 seconds while it uploads

## Step 4: Done
Netlify gives you a live URL like:
```
https://cool-name-12345.netlify.app
```

Share this URL - it's your business website!
